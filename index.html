Dian Zheng

I am currently a second-year master's student at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), where I am advised by Wei-Shi Zheng. Before coming to SYSU, I obtained my B.E. degree at the Dalian University of Technology.

My research interests mainly lie around Computer Vision, with the goal of developing general and generalizable vision systems. Nowadays, I focus on AIGC and their applications for downstream tasks. I believe it is one of the essential tools for facilitating people's interaction with modern vision systems and enhancing the quality of their lives. Now, I am committing to take a small step toward this vision.

I am always open for research discussions and collaborations.
