Dian Zheng

I am currently a second-year master's student at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), where I am advised by Wei-Shi Zheng. Before coming to SYSU, I obtained my B.E. degree at the Dalian University of Technology.

My research interests mainly lie around Computer Vision, with the goal of developing general and generalizable vision systems. My main researchs focus on AIGC and its applications on downstream tasks. I believe it is one of the essential tools for facilitating people's interaction with modern vision systems and enhancing the quality of their lives. Now, I am committing to take a small step toward this vision.

In the following years, I will focus on 2d/3d vision AIGC and efficient AIGC. And I am always open for research discussions and collaborations.
